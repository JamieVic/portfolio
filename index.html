About Me

My name is Jamie, and I'm a predominantly self-taught web developer. I began learning how to write HTML & CSS online, and then participated in courses through high school and tertiary education to improve my skills. Through my studies I've been able to not only learn code but also great techniques to use and making sure the final product is properly tested to ensure a smooth launch.

I'm passionate about web development and try to keep up to date with new practices through W3C and browsing online communities. I want to build websites and other web based programs from scratch that can help individuals and businesses gain exposure and provide interactivity with their customers.

Through my previous job history, I consider myself an interpretive person and will listen to customers wants and needs to be able to provide a solution that suits them. Feel free to browse my portfolio to see what I've recently worked on and get in contact with me if you're interested in working together.
